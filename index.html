dream
Most of my projects begin as imaginations of what can be. Like daydreams in which you float in pure possibility. It is no surprise that my first introduction to writing code came about through the creative coding world which embraces challenging traditional notions of what code and its output can be. While programming is often seen as a very technical skill, approaching it from a place of creativity has been a wonderful bridge allowing me to connect both my technical side with my strong inclination towards expression and creativity. The result -coding being additional medium to share my dreams and ideas with the world.
design
I have a strong inclination towards simplicity and minimalism when it comes to design. I enjoy the challenge of trying to provide as much control and flexibility to projects while staying true to minimalistic principles. This often requires thinking of what components can be paired together or developing UI elements that can perform multiple tasks. My design solutions and ideas do not always meet my highest of aspirations, but with each attempt a new lesson is learned and/or a new approach is conceived.
develop
Tterative development is central to my development process as it allows room for exploring new features without feeling overwhelmed by all my ideas. My imagination tends to be far more expansive than the time I can realistically dedicate to my projects. I`ve also found that despite the perception of creative coding being more form than function the lessons learned from developing my ideas expand far beyond any one project. I've had to learn how to implement various algorithms, use different technologies, refine my problem solving, improve math literacy, and even discover my own unique contributions to problems.
