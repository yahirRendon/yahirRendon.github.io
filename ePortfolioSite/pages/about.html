About

My desire is to use technology to restore abilities to those that have lost them and to create new experiences for those who currently cannot imagine them. In the process I hope to love endlessly, regularly engage in laughter, and perpetually improve upon my abilities.

During the day, I currently work as a Lay Representative advocating for injured workers and helping them navigate the complex industrial insurance policies. It is challenging work in which you must find joy in the opportunities to right wrongs and hold the system accountable. I received a degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Engineering from Southern New Hampshire University. I will be pursuing a graduate degree in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Austin in the fall of 2021. In my free time, I enjoy playing music and exploring various creative endeavors. I look forward to sharing my progress and projects along the way.
