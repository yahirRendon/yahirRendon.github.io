about
This was a simple game I created to help work though some logic and flow elements of a game. There isn't anything special to it but can be fun to see how man levels can be reached or what your high score can be. The score is calculated based on how close you are to the target when it is found and the number of clicks remaining in the round. A final score is calculated when you fail to complete a level by subtracting your running average click distance from the current score. After every three levels completed the number of available clicks decreases by one until you only have 3 clicks available per round. Additionally, trying the hard mode the distance to target is no longer displayed and the player has to observe the pulsing ring navigate their next guess.
