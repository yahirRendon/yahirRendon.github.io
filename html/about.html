about
I aim to be a healthy combination of both a software developer and a creative coder in order to build software applications that make the world easier to access and more beautiful to experience. I greatly appreciate my formal education in standard development practices while also fully embracing my lifelong passion for creative expression in order to offer something unique to every project. I find just as much beauty and talent required to implement an algorithm as I find present in composing music. In the end, my creativity forces me to learn new technical abilities and expanding my technical abilities directly inspires my creative process.
I received a degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Engineering from Southern New Hampshire University. I am always aiming to improve upon my knowledge and abilities through various resources and projects. I am grateful to those that make learning more accessible and engaging through a multitude of platforms. I will pursue continued education formally and informally for the purpose of doing more to find solutions to the challenges before us all.
During the day, I currently work as a Lay Representative advocating for injured workers helping them to navigate complex industrial insurance policies. It is challenging work in which you must find joy in the opportunities to right wrongs that result in clearer skies for those that can only see a brewing storm. In my free time I enjoy creating music, cooking, learning new things, engaging in healthy discourse, and exploring all things technology related. Regardless of what life may bring I hope to love endlessly, regularly engage in laughter, and perpetually improve upon what I can offer to others.
